The Habitat Sigma | Townhouses



"...first of its kind in Gaborone"

Nestled along the boundary of Sarona City and designed to be lived in, designed to be enjoyed, The Habitat Sigma comprises eighteen (18) expertly designed luxury townhouses of double and triple volume.

This will be a quiet, access-controlled community living overlooking the northern horizons of Gaborone with round-the-clock dedicated security and state-of-the-art features such as biometrics, on-site professional health and medical services, and scrumptious restaurants.
The development further boasts proximity to sought-after schools with interlinking roads to major centers of attraction and relaxation in Gaborone.
