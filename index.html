    SARONA CITY

    ...a city within the City


The home you have dreamt of is now a reality with the exciting development of Sarona City, nestled between Limpopo Drive and Segoditshane Way in Tsholofelo East, Gaborone. Sarona City gives quality of life in a unique manner of peace and ease of living including ease of access to lifestyle necessities and free flowing open landscaping in the development.

Sarona City allows for a spectrum of affordable living and is perfect for a lock up and go development. Developments within Sarona City include apartments, town houses, standalone family homes, mall, kindergarten, offices, and an indoor swimming pool.
