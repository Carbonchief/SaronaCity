About

Eco City is an innovative residential and retail development designed to offer contemporary, eco-friendly living in the heart of Gaborone. Blending luxury with nature, Eco City provides high-quality, affordable housing and commercial spaces that reflect its commitment to sustainability and community.

Nestled near Old Naledi, this development is set to empower the surrounding community. Residents and visitors will enjoy access to a 3815 sqm mall, offering a range of retail outlets, as well as a filling station, all designed with convenience and luxury in mind.

Our sophisticated sanctuary combines opulence and practicality, offering serene green spaces where residents can gather, relax, and enjoy a harmonious blend of luxury living and nature.

Whether you are looking for a dream home, a strategic investment, or a place to connect with nature,
Eco City is where opulence and nature embrace.
