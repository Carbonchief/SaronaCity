    SARONA CITY

    ...a city within the City


The home you have dreamt of is now a reality with the exciting development of Sarona City, nestled between Limpopo Drive and Segoditshane Way in Tsholofelo East, Gaborone. Sarona City gives quality of life in a unique manner of peace and ease of living including ease of access to lifestyle necessities and free flowing open landscaping in the development.

Sarona City allows for a spectrum of affordable living and is perfect for a lock up and go development. Developments within Sarona City include apartments, town houses, standalone family homes, mall, kindergarten, offices, and an indoor swimming pool.

The Habitat Kappa

In the heart of Sarona City you will find modern appartments.

The Habitat Kappa is established in a prime location at Tsholofelo East, Sarona City.

The development is within close proximity to Sarona City Mall, the Habisicus Pre-Shool, and Botlhale International School.

The Habitat Kappa encompasses a sleek and rejuvenating exterior and interior design. This convenient environment combined with modernity meets all necessities of life.

Sarona City Mall

The first shopping centre to sit within an estate, Sarona City Shopping Centre will complement the unique lifestyle living of the estate.

The 39 shops of Sarona City Shopping Centre provide an offering designed for uniqueness in a convenient set-up.

Shoppers at Sarona City Shopping Centre will enjoy fine dining, exciting shopping for fashion, hair & beauty, pharmaceuticals, groceries, stationery, with a medical centre and laundromat, and exclusive shops & services.
