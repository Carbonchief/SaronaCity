Tenant Mix

The 39 shops of Sarona City Shopping Centre provide an offering designed for uniqueness in a convenient set-up. Shoppers at Sarona City Shopping Centre will enjoy fine dining, exciting shopping for fashion, hair & beauty, pharmaceuticals, groceries, stationery, with a medical centre and laundromat, and exclusive shops & services.

The first shopping centre to sit within an estate, Sarona City Shopping Centre complements the unique lifestyle living of the estate. The tenant mix, consisting of 39 shops, a filling station and ATM’s, provides the convenient neighbourhood shopping.

Sarona City Shopping Centre is highly visible from Limpopo Drive in Tsholofelo East, with ease of access from Segoditshane Way in Tsholofelo West. It provides a superb convenience shopping to the residents of Ledumang, Marang, Tsogang, Glen Valley, Broadhurst and those en-route to and from Phakalane and Oodi.
